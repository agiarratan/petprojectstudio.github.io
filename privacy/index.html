Nuclear Medicine Tutor does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app operates as an educational tool, and no user accounts, sign-ins, or personal data collection occur within the app.

The app may interact with third-party AI services solely for the purpose of generating educational responses. These services do not receive any user identity or personal information.

No user data is retained, stored, or logged within the app or by PET Project Studio.

This app is intended for adult learners and medical professionals. It is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
